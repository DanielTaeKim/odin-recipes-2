Protein Shake Recipe

Description

Savor the creamy goodness of our 81.5 grams of protein banana protein shake, a revitalizing and nutritious drink that's perfect for any time of the day. In this delectable concoction, ripe bananas lend their natural sweetness, providing a luscious base that's both wholesome and satisfying. Blended to perfection with a scoop of your favorite protein powder, this shake packs a protein punch that'll keep you energized throughout the day. Oatmeal, egg whites, and peanut butter make this the perfect shake for bulking. Whether enjoyed as a post-workout recovery drink or as a replacement meal to meet your protein intake, this banana protein shake is a delicious way to refuel your body while delighting your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup of milk (13.5g protein)
  • 1 cup oatmeal (6g protein)
  • 1/2 cup egg white (13g protein)
  • 1 banana (or any other fruit) (1g protein)
  • 3 tablespoons of peanut butter (12g protein)
  • 2 scoops of whey protein powder (36g protein)

Steps

  1. Simply place all your ingredients in a blender.
  2. Then just blend until whatever thickness you prefer! If a smoother drink is preferred a longer blend may be required in order to blend up the oatmeal.
  3. Drink right away, or save it in your fridge to drink any other time of the day.
